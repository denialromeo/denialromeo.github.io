[h1]Gaming Interests[/h1]

I'm not optimistic about current trends in gaming (micro-transactions, streaming, DLC, etc.), and so spend most of my in-game time in emulators or indie games.

For whatever reason, AAA game quality took a steep nose-dive after around 2004, and even Nintendo hasn't released a truly great title since 2010.

But however dismal the future looks, the past never loses its shine.
