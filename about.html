Investing Rules

Just some quick beliefs that guide my investing acumen –

  • Only invest in stocks or bonds. Real estate, gold, Bitcoin, etc. are all scams long-term.
  • Insider trading is rampant. Never buy individual stocks. Only index funds like VOO.
  • Work out if it's a bull market or a bear market. In a bull market, dollar-cost average into VOO. In a bear market (like present), cash out and buy T-Bills for a 4-5% return over a year.
  • Never go counter-trend. Only invest sincerely in bull markets. Only T-Bills in bear markets.
  • "There is timing in the Way of the merchant, in the rise and fall of capital. All things entail rising  and falling timing. You must be able to discern this." – Miyamoto Musashi

Further bull market rules –

  • Pay attention to the Federal Reserve. In 2021, they sold at the peak. If they sell, you sell. Also pay attention to the Chinese government. They banned Bitcoin at its peak.
  • Also listen to Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger, Ray Dalio, and Jeff Bezos. They are oracles.
  • Feel free to invest 80% of net worth. But follow up with above indicators at least monthly.
  • $500,000 is more than enough to retire to India permanently. Always remember this.
