Political Views

100 years from now, nobody will remember that we existed. Live authentically. I'm a Hindu.

Some deep thoughts –

  • If I had a time machine, I'd try to prevent the Peloponnesian War. It set us back a millennium.
  • China, Japan, and South Korea's populations are now declining. East Asia already peaked.
  • White supremacy and Islamism won't survive demographic shifts in Europe and MENA.
  • Sincere belief in an afterlife is rare. Religion is now mostly political, and politics is a religion.
  • Since 2020, telework, global Internet, and LLMs have transformed society. 2019 feels alien.
  • AI and bots have killed text and image-based social media. Web 2.0 is mostly dead.
  • Popularizing IVF and selecting for more girl children would fix many issues (crime, etc.).
  • A few leaders (Wilson, FDR, LBJ, Reagan, Trump) decide history. We're just along for the ride.
