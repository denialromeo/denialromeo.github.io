Political Views

I'm broadly happy with the direction the U.S. is going, and look forward to a lifetime here.

My goals –

  • A shared American culture based in free speech, self-reliance, and rule of law.
  • U.S. dominance in technology at all levels of the supply chain.
  • Full digitization and automation of all paperwork nationally, with AI checks to catch errors.
  • Trillions in startup subsidies for nuclear power, artificial intelligence, drones, and space travel.
  • Normalization of interracial marriage and an end to informal racial segregation in workplaces.
  • Feminist initiatives to train more women into male-dominated fields.
  • Higher cultural expectations for Americans at all incomes to be well-read and physically fit.
  • A crackdown on Islamist, white supremacist, and CCP-aligned groups.
  • Discarding outmoded Cold War foreign policy for deeper relations with Israel and India.
  • More focus on employment-based immigration and an end to green card country caps.
  • An increase of the U.S. population to one billion by the end of the century.
