Random Acclaimed Song

Since 2001, statistician Henrik Franzon has maintained acclaimedmusic.net, his ranking of the top‑rated songs of all time. This song was randomly chosen from his 2018 list of 10,272 songs.

More Fun

You can limit the song selection by playing with the URL! Examples –

  • Songs released in the 1980's.
  • Songs by Bob Marley.
  • Songs whose titles contain the word "love".
  • Songs by the Beach Boys from the 1960's about surfing.

Also note that each artist's name is a link to their Wikipedia page.
